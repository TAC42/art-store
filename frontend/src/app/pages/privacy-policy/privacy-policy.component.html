Privacy Policy

We at Ori Carlin dedicate ourselves to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy details our practices for collecting, using, sharing, and protecting your personal information across our website, oricarlin.com. By engaging with our website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. This policy is crafted to comply with federal privacy laws applicable within the United States.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you through various interactions on our site, including when you create an account, make a purchase, or contact us. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Order Information: Details of your purchases, including quantities, prices, and transaction specifics (like the payment method).
  • Payment Information: We use third-party payment processors (PayPal and Venmo) to handle payments. While we do not store your credit card details, we retain information about payment status and transaction IDs.
  • User Account Information: Your username, encrypted password, and preferences.

Additionally, we automatically collect information about your visit to our website, such as your IP address and browsing behavior, through cookies and similar technologies.

2. Utilization of Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders: We require your personal and order information to complete transactions, manage deliveries, and provide you with order updates. This process involves sharing necessary details with payment processors and delivery services to ensure the successful fulfillment of your purchases.
  • Communicate with you: Your contact information helps us to respond to your inquiries, send you updates about your account, and inform you of any changes to our services or policies. We strive for clear, timely communication to ensure you’re always in the loop regarding your interactions with Ori Carlin.
  • Improve our products and services: We analyze your interaction with our site and feedback to identify trends and preferences. This data informs our development process, guiding adjustments and enhancements to our offerings. Understanding your needs and preferences helps us create better, more personalized experiences.
  • Comply with legal obligations: Certain laws and regulations mandate the collection and retention of transactional and user information. We use your data to ensure our compliance with these legal requirements, including tax laws, consumer protection laws, and other applicable statutes. This may involve reporting to regulatory authorities or responding to lawful requests for information.

In every step of utilizing your information, we adhere to strict data protection and privacy practices designed to safeguard your personal details. Our commitment to your privacy is unwavering, as we continually assess and update our processes in alignment with best practices and legal standards.

3. Use of Cookies and Session Storage

To enhance your experience on our website and facilitate seamless navigation, Ori Carlin uses cookies and session storage technology. Here’s how and why we use these technologies:

  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device that help us improve our site's functionality, understand user preferences, and optimize our services based on usage patterns. Cookies may track your site navigation, preferences, and activity to tailor your experience.
  • Session Storage: Utilized to manage your session as a logged-in user, session storage temporarily holds data related to your current website visit. It enables a more personalized and coherent experience by remembering your actions and preferences during the session. This information is deleted once you close your browser, ensuring your session data is kept private and not stored long-term.

You have control over the use of cookies and can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

Our use of session storage and cookies is designed with your privacy in mind, aiming to enhance your experience without compromising your personal information. For more details on how we handle data collected by cookies and session storage, please refer to our [Data Security] section.

7. Children's Privacy

In alignment with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), Ori Carlin respects the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. Should a parent or guardian discover that their child has provided us with personal information, we urge you to get in touch with us immediately.

Additionally, our services are designed for a general audience and are not intended for users under the age of 18. We apologize to the younger crowd eager to engage with our content ("sorry, Billy, this means you’ll need your parents to purchase that mug for you!").
